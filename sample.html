ABOUT



E - Vidyalaya is one stop destination for school dropouts which provides primary education. We mainly focus on rural areas of India.Through this portal students learn various subjects which helps them to take up board exams as private candidate. We are trying to give a live experience to students where students can ask their doubts to teachers. Teachers upload videos and post assignments for the students.
